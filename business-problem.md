# Business Problem

Hotel guest communication is frequently distributed across website forms, messaging applications, email, telephone, and online travel platforms.

The operational issue is not only message volume. It is the absence of a consistent process for capturing guest details, identifying intent, responding, logging the interaction, and escalating cases that require a person.

## Manual workflow risks

- delayed responses;
- repeated answers;
- incomplete booking information;
- inconsistent guest communication;
- duplicated data entry;
- limited reporting;
- poor handover between shifts;
- difficulty adding new channels.

## Automation objective

Create a modular guest-service layer that standardises incoming requests while allowing every channel to keep its own trigger and response mechanism.

The automation should support routine requests without pretending to replace hotel staff in sensitive, urgent, payment-related, or exceptional situations.
